# Deity Types
There are three types of deities within and around Linaria.

## Celestial deities
These are the most mortal-appearing of the deities. They easily blend into mortal surroundings and are often depicted as being part of mortal communities. Their worshippers typically form structured groups with priests leading a congregation.

## Demonic deities
These deities have corporeal forms that do not resemble mortals in any way, often with mismatched or missing features and terrifying visages. While they have walked amongst mortals, they are more likely to be found on the fringes. Their worshippers typically form semi-structured groups with a charismatic person leading almost solely by force of personality and coercion.

## Mystic forces
At times, a “force” will become worshipped as if it were a deity. These are wholly incorporeal beings with no structured following. The worship of mystic forces is a purely personal and individual experience.
