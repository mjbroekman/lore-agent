# Magic
Magic in Linaria is common, though not as common as in Zhiedphis itself. Across Linaria, there are almost as many magical traditions as there are communities, ranging from arcane schools to divine temples or nature shrines. Anyone who wishes to may study magic, and their growth depends solely on the effort they put into their study and practice, and the quality of the materials they use to perform their acts.

However, high-level magic is rare. Spells above 3rd level must be discovered or bargained for to learn/use.

Casters that gain their powers from extra-planar sources must make an ability check of DC 13 + spell level to prepare spells of 3rd level or above. Lower-level spells can be up-leveled without issue.
