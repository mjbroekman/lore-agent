# Maethirion
[Maethirion] is the first deity created by [Ai Xiang] and a father figure within the [Zhiedphisian Pantheon].

# Title
The Father

# Type
Demonic

# Symbol
A feather quill

# Appearance
[Maethirion] appears in most representations as a horned male with five breasts (four on chest, one on back) and four bloody, charred, but feathered wings. Each breast is tattooed with a different glyph.
