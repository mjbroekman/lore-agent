# Ai Xiang
[Ai Xiang] is the matriarch and head of the [Zhiedphisian Pantheon].

# Title
The Mother

# Type
Celestial

# Symbol
Two hands with palms touching, one on top of the other, facing in opposite directions

# Appearance
[Ai Xiang] appears in most representations as an old woman in black ropes, carrying star-embroidered cloth veils.
